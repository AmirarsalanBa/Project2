OrbitXPay FAQ

Who is eligible for an OrbitXPay card?

Any web3 business or individual is eligible.

How long does the KYC process take? What documents are needed?

The KYC process can take anywhere between 15 minutes to 48 hours, depending on the individual profile.

Required documents:

  • For individuals: ID and proof of residence
  • For businesses: Certificate of incorporation and personal KYC documents for Ultimate Beneficial Owners (UBOs)

How much time before I can start using the card?

You can start using the card immediately upon completion of KYC/KYB (Know Your Customer/Know Your Business).

How long does it take for the physical card to reach?

Delivery time for the physical card ranges from 1 to 4 weeks, depending on your location.

How does OrbitXPay stand out in the market?

OrbitXPay distinguishes itself through several key features:
  1. Offers both credit and debit options against your crypto.
  2. Allows flexible switching between credit and debit usage.
  3. Provides unlimited off-ramping with no set limits.
  4. Uniquely offers Visa Signature and Infinite cards in its product lineup.
